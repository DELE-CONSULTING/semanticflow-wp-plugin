Track and analyze AI bot visits to your WordPress.

== Description ==

SemanticFlow Tracker helps you monitor and analyze visits from AI-powered bots and LLM crawlers to your WordPress website. With the growing importance of AI search engines and chatbots, understanding how these services interact with your content is crucial.
